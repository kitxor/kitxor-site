Mafia: Definitive Edition - A Quick Reflection

2025-12-29 | gaming

What is it

Remake of 2002 classic mob story set in 1930s Lost Heaven. Picked it up after RDR2, heard it was GTA-style with mob storyline.

Official Game Link

What worked

  • Fast paced, hop from chapter to chapter
  • Vehicle control is good
  • 1930s vibe is authentic
  • Loved Pauli's arc, our main guy Tommy not so much

What didn't

  • Combat mechanics are poor, I had a hard time ducking while shooting
  • Linear story, should have had more depth
  • Interesting characters but unexplored

Memorable moments

Don Salieri and Paulie carried the story. Everyone else forgettable.

Worth playing?

Yes, if you want a fast-paced story-driven shooter set in the 1930s.

My time to beat: 15 hours
